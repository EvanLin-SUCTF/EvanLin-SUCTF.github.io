Happy birthday

Posted Nov 23, 2024
By EvanLin
2 min read

Nov 23, 2024

Today is my birthday. I didn’t really plan to celebrate it in any special way—just another normal day. When I woke up this morning, I noticed a few messages from friends wishing me a happy birthday. It was a small thing, but it definitely made the morning feel a bit warmer.

Work was pretty normal. A couple of coworkers found out it was my birthday and insisted on getting lunch together. Nothing fancy, just a simple meal and some casual conversation. They joked about me getting older, which I guess is unavoidable every year.

After work I headed home earlier than usual. I stopped by a small bakery on the way and bought a slice of cake for myself. It felt a little funny celebrating alone, but at the same time it was actually quite relaxing. Sometimes a quiet evening is exactly what you need.

Later at night I spent some time browsing a few tech blogs and organizing some notes I’ve been writing recently. I’ve been trying to keep things documented more regularly—ideas, small projects, random thoughts.

Birthdays used to feel like a big event when I was younger. These days they’re a lot simpler, but maybe that’s not a bad thing. A calm day, a few messages from friends, and some time to myself feels like a pretty decent way to mark another year.

— 2024年11月23日

今天是我的生日，其实没有打算特别庆祝，只是普通的一天。早上醒来时看到朋友发来的几条生日祝福消息，虽然只是简单的问候，但还是让人感觉心情很好。

白天工作和平时差不多，有同事知道是我生日，就一起出去简单吃了顿午饭。大家一边聊天一边开玩笑说我又老了一岁，不过气氛倒是挺轻松的。

下班后我比平时早一点回家，路过一家小面包店的时候顺便买了一块小蛋糕。一个人简单吃点东西，反而感觉挺放松的。

晚上花了一点时间看看技术博客，也整理了一下最近写的笔记。最近在尝试把一些想法和项目记录下来，感觉慢慢养成这个习惯还挺不错的。

小时候的生日总觉得很隆重，现在反而变得简单很多。不过这样平静的一天，其实也挺好的。
