Play with me t_t

Posted Jun 3, 2025
By EvanLin
2 min read

June 3, 2025

Today after work I suddenly felt like playing some Minecraft. It’s been a while since I last logged in, and for some reason the idea just popped into my head while I was on the way home.

Playing alone can get a bit boring sometimes, so I messaged 2hi5hu and asked if he wanted to join later in the evening. At first he said “maybe”, which I took as a yes. I spent a little time preparing a small area in the world and waited for him to show up.

Well… he never did.

By the time it got late there was still no sign of him online. I guess he must have gotten busy with something else or simply forgot. In the end I just explored the world alone for a while, mining a bit and walking around the base.

At some point I had a random idea and built a tiny “memorial” near the spawn point with a sign that simply said: “Here lies 2hi5hu, who promised to play but never arrived.”

It was mostly a joke, but it did make the empty world feel a little less quiet. Maybe next time he’ll actually show up.

—

2025年6月3日

今天下班之后突然有点想玩 Minecraft。好像很久没有上线了，在回家的路上不知道为什么突然就想起来了。

一个人玩有时候会有点无聊，于是就顺手问了一下 2hi5hu 晚上要不要一起上线。他当时回了个“可能吧”，我就当他是答应了。于是我先上服务器稍微整理了一下附近的区域，等他上线。

结果等到晚上挺晚了，人影都没见到。

大概是临时有别的事情，或者干脆忘了吧。最后只能自己一个人随便逛了逛世界，挖了点矿，在基地附近转了转。

后来突然有个很无聊的想法，就在出生点旁边简单堆了个小“墓碑”，放了个牌子写着：“这里埋葬着 2hi5hu —— 说好要来玩但从没出现的人。”

虽然只是开个玩笑，但多少让这个空荡荡的世界看起来没那么冷清了。希望下次他真的能上线吧。
